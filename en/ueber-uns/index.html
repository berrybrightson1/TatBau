About TAT Bau

TAT Bau is your trusted partner in Delmenhorst for high-quality building elements around your home. We offer aluminum entrance doors, windows, shutters, and sun protection solutions for both residential and commercial properties. Our approach combines personal consultation with precise craftsmanship—delivering results that not only look great but also perform reliably in the long term.

What we stand for

At TAT Bau, you won't get off-the-shelf solutions. Every project is tailored to your specific needs. From the initial consultation and measurements to the final installation, we focus on precision, high-quality materials, and clean workmanship. Our goal is to ensure you feel well advised and remain satisfied for years to come.

Regional and close to you

From our location in Delmenhorst, we support projects throughout the Bremen region and beyond. Whether it's a new build or a renovation, we guide you from planning to final installation. We work with both private and commercial clients, providing reliable solutions that truly work in everyday life.
